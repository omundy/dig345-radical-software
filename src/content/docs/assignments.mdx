## Final Project 

Technology has always been complicated. It embodies the dreams as much as the failures of our species. It accelerates the possibility we might improve and ease the burdens of humankind, yet continues to allow us to cause harm and inequality. The tech that Facebook creates is considered innovative, not because it makes the world better, safer, or more humane, but due to its ability to capture attention, manipulate populations, and generate massive wealth for a few individuals. 

Recall however, technology is not only not neutral, it is also not yet self-deterministic. The messiness of technology mirrors our own possibilities and biases. The definition of "new" has equal subjectivity. The invention of the wheel, surely a big deal at its "launch party," allows humans unprecedented mobility as much as it accelerates us in our path to destroy the planet. 

Using the technology and context(s) from this course, you will create a final project is a departure from common functions of software.
