## Tutorial 

1. Read [Browser Blowup: Explode Web Pages Containing Third-Party Trackers](https://owenmundy.com/site/explode-the-web) (2019).
1. Follow the updated [Explode the Web!](https://sneakaway-studio.github.io/explode-the-web/tutorial/slides/0-introduction.html) tutorial to learn how to build your own browser extension. 
    1. [Introduction](https://sneakaway-studio.github.io/explode-the-web/tutorial/slides/0-introduction.html)
    1. [Hello, World!](https://sneakaway-studio.github.io/explode-the-web/tutorial/slides/1-hello-world.html)
    1. [Extension architecture](https://sneakaway-studio.github.io/explode-the-web/tutorial/slides/2-architecture.html)
    1. [DOM and browser actions](https://sneakaway-studio.github.io/explode-the-web/tutorial/slides/3-dom.html)
    1. [Explosion](https://sneakaway-studio.github.io/explode-the-web/tutorial/slides/4-explosion.html)
1. Compare versions in the [repo](https://github.com/sneakaway-studio/explode-the-web/)
1. Review [published](https://sneakaway-studio.github.io/explode-the-web/tutorial/slides/5-examples.html) and example browser extensions (below)
